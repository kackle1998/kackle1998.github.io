Key Projects

Patent-Pending Medical Device with Two-Chamber Extrusion Mechanism

Product Development Mechanical Design FDA Compliance Precision Engineering

Led the complete design lifecycle of a patent-pending medical device for in-situ wound repair during larynx surgery, featuring a two-chamber extrusion system for controlled medicine delivery and precise dosage through a ratcheting trigger and gear train mechanism.

  • Designed initial prototype from concept through detailed engineering
  • Developed ratcheting trigger and gear train for controlled, precise medicine extrusion
  • Optimized chamber geometry for consistent flow and surgical precision
  • Led multiple design iterations based on testing feedback
  • Ensured FDA regulatory compliance and managed patent documentation
  • Coordinated cross-functional development team to achieve design goals

Tools Used:

SolidWorks CAD SolidWorks CFD Ansys Structural Simulator AutoCAD
Impact: Created a novel, patent-pending medical device advancing wound repair capabilities in laryngeal surgery with precise medicine delivery
