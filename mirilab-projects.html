Medical Device Research & Design Engineering

Surgical Hydrogel Extruder

Led the research and development of an innovative two-chamber medicine extrusion device designed for larynx surgery applications. This patent-pending medical device delivers controlled dosages through a sophisticated mechanical system combining ergonomics, precision engineering, and FDA compliance.

The device features a ratcheting trigger mechanism coupled with a gear train to provide mechanical advantage, enabling precise control during surgical procedures. The project spanned the complete product development lifecycle from initial concept through validated prototypes ready for clinical evaluation.
